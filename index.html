About StemBia

StemBia is a STEM focused nonprofit upcoming initiative that uses technology and engineering solutions to solve real-life challenges facing the Gambia. The whole concept of StemBia is based on research. At StemBia, we make research on problem domains and find the best possible solutions for solving the problem by combining technology and engineering tools and concept.
At StemBia, we welcome all enthusiast students from high school to university, with a deep understanding of mathematics, physics or computer programming.
The founder believe that being expose to these experiences at an early age, will help ignite more creativity from students. The founder is a tech geek and coding enthusiast with a deep passion for scientific research, his involvement in research activities from an early age made him realize that we can do better by making best use of concepts taught in school, his currently a high school sophomore at Nusrat High School.
